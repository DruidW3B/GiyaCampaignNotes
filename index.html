Session IThe Carnival (DM Notes)▾
TL;DR

The party's first night out, a visit to the Dark Fire Circus that turns to chaos when goblin raiders storm the Big Top. Knights of L'Equip cover the retreat, and the party glimpses a massive, red-eyed goblin leader before choosing whether to leave with Knight Commander Joseph Kiliman or chase the goblins and their prisoners. -Claude

Party at level 1.

The Dark Fire Circus

  • The party starts at the Dark Fire Circus, following a brief introduction of how and why each character ended up there, then eases in with gambling and carnival games.
  • The attractions and their proprietors:
    • Food from the Nobleshnap brothers of the GooeyPork Tavern franchise
    • Fortune telling by Jyasinta Helvitar
    • Ring Da BellZ, run by Azlugg the Mighty
    • Target Practice, run by Grizzle Tooth Snagger
    • Trissinda's Tea Room, serving tea with an optional leaf reading
    • Shipple Shabangs' Find the Fairy Cup game
    • The Wheel of Fortune, run by Theradeas Toplledoppolos, one of the Toplledoppolos brothers who own the Gooey Pork Tavern chain
    • Dr Simon Corvus's potion stall, whimsical potions from a man who seems a little off

The Big Top

  • Once the party tires of the games, they are ushered into the Big Top: clowns, animal tamers, acrobats, and the wild magics of Elbafore Critterspeaker and his fish in a bubble, Jeramiah.
  • Elbafore announces the arrival of Lady Alrina Kazadina, a powerful druid who speaks to animals to entertain the crowd.

The Raid

  • Toward the end of the show, noises rise outside and the Big Top is swarmed by goblin raiders. The party and a few NPCs either flee or stand and fight.
  • If Lady Alrina falls, her mammoth turns feral and attacks anyone nearby unless Elbafore can calm it.
  • The party is nearly overrun until knights from the nearby bastion of L'Equip arrive to cover their retreat.

The Escape

  • As they leave, they glimpse Jyasinta teleporting herself to safety, and see the goblin leader: massive for a goblin, glowing red eyes, screaming orders to his subordinates.
  • The party can either leave with Knight Commander Joseph Kiliman or chase the goblins, who are making off with prisoners.

Ended session at level 1.

Session IIJackson the Bull Kills Goblins (DM Notes)▾
TL;DR

Chasing the goblin shaman to free the circus prisoners, the party befriends a bull named Jackson who tears through the goblin camp. They rescue Elbafore and learn the sacrifices aim to summon not a spider god but one of The Dark Pantheon, the red giant watching Lance, later known as Volmol. Ezekiel meets The Collector on the road home, and after the shaman falls, Jeros nearly wipes the party with its staff. Back at the fortress, they later expose and kill the treacherous Jafar, with Hella (Red Jasmin) landing the final blow, and Eris claims her first sword shard. -Claude

Party at level 2.

Before the Hunt

  • After Session 1, the party decides to chase down the goblin shaman to save any prisoners taken from the circus.
  • Each of them experiences a vision or dream involving a parent, a loved one, or a darker entity entirely.
  • Before leaving, they can speak with Duchess Marine, who hints that all is not what it seems in L'Equip. Talking with several NPCs turns up clues about who and what is causing trouble in the fortress.
  • Just before reaching the camp, the party's druid, Prickly Khactuz, befriended a bull named Jackson.

The Goblin Camp

  • The party approaches the goblin camp and is thrown straight into a fight as goblins swarm them. Jackson the bull proves a formidable asset, slaying many goblins himself.
  • After the battle, they can rescue Elbafore from a nearby cave, who helps heal the party before they move on to face the shaman.
  • They have a chance to save some of the carnival folk.

The Shaman

  • A vision reveals what the goblins are trying to summon with their sacrifices. It is not the spider god everyone assumed, but one of The Dark Pantheon: the god watching Lance specifically, a large, red, menacing figure peering down from the clouds. The party later identified it in Session 3 as Volmol.

The Journey Back

  • Camping on the way back, Ezekiel is visited by a mysterious woman offering instant fame and fortune in exchange for his eternal soul. When he refuses, she reveals herself as The Collector, a nemesis-like figure for the party.

Aftermath

  • The party succeeded in killing the goblin shaman.
  • Jeros picked up the shaman's staff and waved it around, nearly causing a total party kill and coming close to killing Lance and Eris. His chaotic streak here set the tone for how the party treated Jeros from then on.
  • They also took the ritual book, though the DM's note trails off on what Lance did with it.

Session 2.5 (Red Jasmin and Jafar Scene)

  • These notes are written by Web, so they are exactly how this all went down. In no way are any of these details incorrect. (insert sarcasm)
  • The party had a brief conversation with Jafar at the end of Session 1, where he tried to use his charisma on Hella and failed, leaving the party sketched out by him.
  • After Session 2, the party gathered more evidence that he is guilty of treachery and searched the castle for him, finding him in the throne room attacking Duchess Marine. The party killed him, with Hella (Red Jasmin) landing the final blow while Prickly, in parrot form, forced crackers into his mouth, taunting him: "Do you want a cracker, huh? Polly want a cracker!"
  • Eris Thornveil got her first sword shard, starting her on her quest to retrieve them all.

Ended session at level 2.

Session IIIThe Library▾
TL;DR

A lengthy lore dump reveals that 70 years ago six dark gods joined forces to turn the world into a playground of destruction, each one now tied to a party member. Prickly falls for a book, and the royal lady tasks them with finding a cannon. -Claude

Party at level 3.

  • The party finds out much in the lore dump of the century.
  • 70 years ago, 6 dark gods attempted to break into the world. They normally are solo, but this time they joined together to have the planet as a playground of destruction. It's a possibility they have agents on the planet.
    • Saval: temptress, red horns, ambition
    • Bilogis: famine, green
    • Vugak: clown, laughter and trickery
    • Volmol: big red one, war and destruction
    • Izmataz: god of knowledge
    • Imotet: god of death
  • Each of these deities is responsible for and connected to many of the players:
    • Saval is assumed to be affecting Eris.
    • Bilogis is assumed to be affecting Hella.
    • Vugak is assumed to be affecting Ezekial.
    • Volmol is assumed to be affecting Lance.
    • Izmataz is assumed to be affecting no one. Prickly is positive he is not being affected. The DM laughed for some unrelated reason.
    • Imotet is assumed to be affecting Jeros, although that is not specifically said in game.
  • Prickly falls in love with a book and is eventually dragged out of the library with book in hand.
  • They learn from the royal lady they need to find a cannon.

Ended session at level 3.

Session IXBeneath the Tide Pool▾
TL;DR

Orphelia sends the party down a new tide pool into a cavern network. They parley with rat-humanoids mining a hazardous green rock under Sloppy Crookshanks, then trail dark elves to Darahan the Black's slave-mining lair, staffed by the enchantress who erased Jeros's memories. After a messy fight and a spider-form scouting run, they retreat to plan and seek Orphelia's counsel. -Claude

Party at level 5.

At the Tavern

  • The party received a mission from Orphelia to investigate a suspicious tide pool (not seen before).

Rats in the Pool

  • The party discovers the large tide pool is giving access to a trap door.
  • The party investigates to find a secret mining operation of rat-like humanoids.
  • After a tense, hesitant agreement to talk, pushed for by Prickly due to the cuteness of the rat-like humanoids (blame him if all goes wrong), the group finds this operation is run by a commander named Sloppy Crookshanks (Ratman leader).
  • They are after this green shining rock, which seems to have very hazardous properties to the touch and, when consumed, gives the rats powerful abilities.
  • Sloppy Crookshanks mentions others being in the cavern network.
    • Some barbarians that are extremely confrontational.
    • As well as some shady dark elves.
    • Both seem to be somewhat of an uneasy annoyance for Sloppy Crookshanks, but not yet a problem given their undisclosed location.

On the Dark Elves' Trail

  • The party agrees to investigate these elves, assuming they are part of the same faction as Prince Darahan the Black.
  • Going through the network of tunnels, the party sneaks by most guards.
  • They pass by a pool seemingly infected with dead bodies.
    • Later discovered to be the domain of a very large basilisk or other large serpent-like creature (a beast technically, but can't be tamed by Prickly).

Battle

  • The party fights and kills some guards, leading others away from the battle with some odd tactics and DM mercy.
  • The snake comes out to say hi. Prickly offers the bodies of the freshly dead dark elves as, uh, a peace offering.

Investigation of the Dark Elf Lair

  • Prickly investigates the lair in spider form and reports a slave mining operation with a frightening number of slaves and soldiers working for Darahan the Black. Darahan is on the 2nd level in some office, along with the enchantress now responsible for the erasing of Jeros's memories and his conviction for the stolen parrot.

The Verdict

  • The party agrees to back away safely for now to avoid death and formulate a better plan of attack or action, eventually hoping to seek guidance from Orphelia.

## Session 9: Beneath the Tide Pool
> **TL;DR:** Orphelia sends the party down a new tide pool into a cavern network. They parley with rat-humanoids mining a hazardous green rock under Sloppy Crookshanks, then trail dark elves to Darahan the Black's slave-mining lair, staffed by the enchantress who erased Jeros's memories. After a messy fight and a spider-form scouting run, they retreat to plan and seek Orphelia's counsel. -Claude
> **Level:** 5

### At the Tavern
- The party received a mission from Orphelia to investigate a suspicious tide pool (not seen before).

### Rats in the Pool
- The party discovers the large tide pool is giving access to a trap door.
- The party investigates to find a secret mining operation of rat-like humanoids.
- After a tense, hesitant agreement to talk, pushed for by Prickly due to the cuteness of the rat-like humanoids (blame him if all goes wrong), the group finds this operation is run by a commander named Sloppy Crookshanks (Ratman leader).
- They are after this green shining rock, which seems to have very hazardous properties to the touch and, when consumed, gives the rats powerful abilities.
- Sloppy Crookshanks mentions others being in the cavern network.
  - Some barbarians that are extremely confrontational.
  - As well as some shady dark elves.
  - Both seem to be somewhat of an uneasy annoyance for Sloppy Crookshanks, but not yet a problem given their undisclosed location.

### On the Dark Elves' Trail
- The party agrees to investigate these elves, assuming they are part of the same faction as Prince Darahan the Black.
- Going through the network of tunnels, the party sneaks by most guards.
- They pass by a pool seemingly infected with dead bodies.
  - Later discovered to be the domain of a very large basilisk or other large serpent-like creature (a beast technically, but can't be tamed by Prickly).

### Battle
- The party fights and kills some guards, leading others away from the battle with some odd tactics and DM mercy.
- The snake comes out to say hi. Prickly offers the bodies of the freshly dead dark elves as, uh, a peace offering.

### Investigation of the Dark Elf Lair
- Prickly investigates the lair in spider form and reports a slave mining operation with a frightening number of slaves and soldiers working for Darahan the Black. Darahan is on the 2nd level in some office, along with the enchantress now responsible for the erasing of Jeros's memories and his conviction for the stolen parrot.

### The Verdict
- The party agrees to back away safely for now to avoid death and formulate a better plan of attack or action, eventually hoping to seek guidance from Orphelia.
- On the way back, the party prepares to investigate the barbarians in the caverns.
- The session ends with a long rest due to the level up.

*Ended session at level 5.*
